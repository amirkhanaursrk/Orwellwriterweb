  • Orwell Writer is a text writing app that was created for the purpose of helping those that strive to improve and enrich their writing. For each word you type following with a blank space, Orwell Writer's suggestions will appear on the right side, under their respective Adjectives, Synonyms, and Predeccessors areas. The export section is comprised of a save as and disfunctional print feature. They work as any other appliction would.
